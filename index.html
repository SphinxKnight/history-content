Editing history of the MDN pages

This history manager groups the main changes that are made or to be made regarding pages in the MDN Web Docs.

Table of page changes

You will find in this table the various documents currently translated, coloured in green or yellow according to two specific criteria allowing to identify if the pages are up to date or are late compared to the American version.

This is described as follows:

  • Green: The document has been modified after the original version.
  • Yellow: The document is potentially not up-to-date.
  • Red: The document is missing.
